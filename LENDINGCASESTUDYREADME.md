- Backgroud of the project: working for an online loan marketplace, facilitating personal loans, business loans, and financing of medical procedures. Borrowers can easily access lower interest rate loans through a fast online interface. 
- Problem to be solved - To remove the risky elements or lenders
- dataset - All the loans issued from 2007-2011
